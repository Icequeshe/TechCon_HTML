Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of tech enthusiasts and has grown into one of the most anticipated tech events globally. Over the years, it has hosted groundbreaking talks, product launches, and interactive workshops that shape the future of technology.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and connect the brightest minds in the tech industry. We aim to provide a platform that accelerates growth for individuals and companies alike.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

A pioneer in computing, Dr. Lovelace spoke at TechCon 2015 on the future of algorithms and AI. Her insights continue to influence the field of computer science.

Elon Turing

Elon Turing, a visionary in autonomous systems, shared his groundbreaking research in robotics during TechCon 2018.

Grace Hopper

At TechCon 2020, Grace Hopper captivated the audience with her insights on software architecture and inclusive leadership in tech.
